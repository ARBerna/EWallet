Welcome!

Welcome to EWallet! Here you can see how each of our pages works

  • Income
    • Display your total amount of money
    • Show your income per month
    • Add/Modify/Delete income entries
  • Expenses
    • Display your total amount of money
    • Show your spending per month
    • Add/Modify/Delete expense entries
  • Planning
    • Display your total amount of money put into your plan
    • Show how much you have added to the plan per month
    • Show how much more you have to add to meet your goal
    • Add/Modify/Delete plan entries
  • Summary
    • Display a summary of your financial status
    • Show your income, expenses, and balance
    • Provide insights into your financial habits
